Introduction

Overview of the mobile app

Introducing our innovative mobile application designed to revolutionize the educational landscape in Kenya. Tailored to navigate the complexities of the current Competence-Based Curriculum (CBC), our app empowers parents to effortlessly assess their students' progress. With user-friendly features, it provides valuable insights, bridging the gap between home and school for a more informed and collaborative educational journey.

Our innovative app is tailored to empower parents within the framework of Kenya's new Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC) system. By providing a user-friendly interface, it enables parents to actively engage in the assessment of their students' progress, fostering a collaborative approach between home and school. With features designed to align with CBC methodologies, our app offers real-time insights into a child's competency development, facilitating informed discussions and personalized support for a well-rounded educational journey.

Purpose and goals of the app

  1. Empowerment Through Assessment:
    • Purpose:Kurasa Mobile aims to empower parents by providing them with the tools to actively assess their students' progress within the CBC system.
    • Goal: Enable parents to take an active role in their child's education, fostering a sense of involvement and empowerment.
  2. Bridge Between Home and School:
    • Purpose:Serve as a bridge between home and school to enhance communication and collaboration between parents and educators.
    • Goal:Facilitate seamless information exchange, ensuring parents are well-informed about their child's educational journey.
  3. Comprehensive Performance Insights:
    • Purpose:Provide parents with detailed insights into their student's academic performance.
    • Goal:Enable parents to make informed decisions by offering a comprehensive view of their child's strengths, weaknesses, and overall progress.
  4. Dynamic Activities View:
    • Purpose: Keep parents engaged with a dynamic Activities View, offering a real-time snapshot of their student's engagements and achievements.
    • Goal:Enhance parental awareness and appreciation of the various activities and accomplishments their child is involved in at school.
  5. Timely and Relevant Notifications:
    • Purpose:Keep parents informed about important updates, events, and milestones in their student's academic journey.
    • Goal:Ensure that parents stay connected and engaged with their child's educational experience by receiving timely and relevant notifications.
  6. Efficient Assignment Management:
    • Purpose:Empower students to excel by providing parents with easy access to assignments and a user-friendly interface for completion.
    • Goal:Facilitate a smooth and efficient process for parents to support their child's learning journey by managing assignments collaboratively through the app.

Target audience

  • parents

Report Generation

The report feature in Kurasa Mobile is designed to offer parents a detailed and intuitive exploration of their student's academic performance. With this feature, parents gain access to comprehensive insights that go beyond mere grades, providing a holistic understanding of their child's strengths, weaknesses, and overall progress within the Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC) system.

How to interprate report entiries

Report screen comprises of several componets:
  • Student Profile:This component indicate the student details and onclick it navigate to a screen that contains more information about the selected student.
  • Student List of Grade: it indicate the student list of current and previous grades each grade has separate generated report.
  • Learning Area: These are the several subject the the student is taking while in school.Different learning areas have different reports to.
  • Strand: it indicate a specific topic of a given learning area.
  • Sub Strand: it indicate a specific sub topic of a given strand(topic).
  • Indicator: This is what the student have been able to achive under the specified topic.
  • Report Files: This section provides the capability to view the student general report or even download it as a PDF

Notification

In the Kurasa Mobile app, the Notifications feature serves as a vital communication tool, ensuring that parents are consistently well-informed about crucial aspects of their student's academic journey. This feature is designed to deliver timely and relevant updates, keeping parents abreast of important events, milestones, and any noteworthy developments in their child's educational experience. By providing a seamless flow of information, the Notifications feature enhances parental engagement, fostering a strong connection between home and school. Parents can expect to receive alerts on various aspects, such as upcoming events, assessment results, achievements, and other significant updates, contributing to a more informed and supportive educational partnership.
