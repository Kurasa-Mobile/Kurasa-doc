Introduction Last updated: 2023-12-15

Introducing our innovative mobile application designed to revolutionize the educational landscape in Kenya. Tailored to navigate the complexities of the current Competence-Based Curriculum (CBC), our app empowers parents to effortlessly assess their students' progress. With user-friendly features, it provides valuable insights, bridging the gap between home and school for a more informed and collaborative educational journey.

Kurasa Mobile Application, crafted by Kurasa—an EdTech pioneer rooted in Nairobi, Kenya—aims to redefine the educational landscape through technology. Our primary focus is to enhance the education system, using innovative solutions that make learning more accessible and impactful

Embarking on a digital odyssey, Kurasa Mobile emerged in 2023, bringing a splash of innovation to the tech scene. Get ready to dive into a realm of possibilities as we redefine mobile experiences and set new standards in the palm of your hand

At Kurasa we are driven by the following values:
  1. The urgency to deliver the best to impart the upcoming generations.
  2. We are constantly learning and work to ensure learning never stops for the students we serve.

Mobile Usage

Now, let's explore the dynamic ways in which this revolutionary app is reshaping education and empowering parents

App Usage:
  1. Parental Assessment: Kurasa Mobile empowers parents to assess their students' progress effectively. The app serves as a bridge between home and school, offering insights into their child's educational journey
  2. Report: Explore comprehensive insights into your student's performance with intuitive and detailed reports
  3. Feeds : Stay in the loop with a dynamic Activities View, providing a real-time snapshot of your student's engagements and accomplishments
  4. Notifications: Receive timely and relevant notifications, ensuring you're always informed about important updates, events, and milestones in your student's academic journey
  5. Assignments: Empower your child to excel by easily accessing and completing assignments within the app's user-friendly interface

Mobile Features

Installation

Kurasa Mobile app is readily available on both Play Store and App Store, making it convenient for users on Android and iOS platforms.

Play Store Installation

To get started with play store installation Get the application by clicking this.

The follow the below steps

Installation Process
  1. Connect to internet: Connect your device to a strong internet - WIFI or mobile data
  2. Locate app from play store: Click on the link above to access the app from play store
  3. Initiate installation: Click on "Install" button to begin the download and intallation process
  4. Open the app: Once installed tap on open button or find the kurasa mobile icon on your home screen and tap to launch the app
  5. Login or sign up:Existing users can log in with their credentials, for new users sign up for an account.

App Store Installation

Authentication Process

Kurasa application have an email based authentication process all you have to do is as follows---.

  • For registerd users access login and enter your credentials i.e emali and password.
  • For unregisterd users access sign up and enter the requierd credentials, verify your account from your email then login using the registerd credentials.
  • Onboarding screens are displayed for the first time otherwise it will alwaysbe feeds screen
